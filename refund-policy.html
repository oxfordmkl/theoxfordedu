Refund & Cancellation Policy

Effective Date: 01 January 2026

At The Oxford Computers, we aim to maintain transparency and fairness in all admission and fee-related matters. This Refund & Cancellation Policy outlines the conditions under which refunds or cancellations may be considered.

1. Course Fees

Course fees once paid are generally non-refundable, as admission confirms seat allocation, faculty planning, and academic resource preparation.

2. Refund Eligibility

Refunds may be considered only under the following circumstances:

  • Course cancellation by The Oxford Computers
  • Duplicate or excess payment made by mistake

All refund requests must be submitted in writing within 7 days from the date of payment.

3. Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds will not be provided in the following cases:

  • Change of mind after admission confirmation
  • Non-attendance or irregular attendance of classes
  • Partial completion of a course
  • Personal, medical, or financial reasons

4. Cancellation by the Institute

If a course is cancelled by The Oxford Computers due to unforeseen or unavoidable circumstances, eligible fees will be refunded or adjusted towards another course, as deemed appropriate by the institute.
