Your Path to Digital Success

Oxford Computers, Malayinkeezhu is a trusted and government-authorized computer education and skill development center, approved by Kerala State Rutronix. We are committed to empowering students, job seekers, and professionals with practical, industry-relevant digital skills that lead to real career opportunities.

With years of experience in computer training and technology services, Oxford Computers focuses on hands-on learning, real-time projects, and career-oriented courses designed to meet the demands of today's job market — covering computer applications, programming, web design, digital marketing, accounting, data entry, and emerging technologies like AI.

We believe that education should be practical, affordable, and accessible. Our experienced trainers provide personalized attention, ensuring that even beginners gain confidence and mastery in their chosen field. Along with technical training, we support students through career guidance, project assistance, certification, and placement support.

Government Authorized Training Center

The Oxford Computers is a government-authorized computer education institute operating as an Authorized Training Centre (ATC) under Kerala State Rutronix. We provide classroom-based and guided computer training programs for students, job seekers, and professionals in Trivandrum and surrounding regions.

Women-Owned & Community-Driven

Proudly women-owned, The Oxford Computers has been a cornerstone of technology education in Malayinkeezhu since 2008, serving students from Kattakada, Neyyattinkara, Balaramapuram, Pappanamcode and across South Kerala.

Our Commitment

All courses are educational services delivered through structured offline and blended learning models. We focus on quality education, practical skills development, and career readiness for every student who walks through our doors.
