About Oxford Computers

Who We Are

Oxford Computers is a Government Approved Study Centre under Kerala State Rutronix. With years of excellence in computer education, we empower students with job-ready skills in IT, programming, digital marketing, and data management.

Our Mission

To deliver affordable, high-quality computer education that bridges the gap between academic learning and industry needs.

Our Vision

To become a premier hub of digital learning that supports every student in achieving career success through modern technology.
